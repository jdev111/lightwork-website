Before You Commit

10 red flags that signal a sick home

These are issues standard home inspections won't catch. Inspectors aren't looking at the home through a health lens. Our New Home Screening will.

1

Overcomplicated rooflines

Complex roof shapes trap moisture and create leak-prone junctures that breed hidden mold.

2

Attached garage

Car exhaust, stored chemicals, and combustion gases migrate into living spaces through shared walls.

3

Older wiring (knob and tube)

Produces elevated electromagnetic fields and can create body voltage in sleeping areas.

4

Crawlspace with HVAC

Mold, moisture, and contaminants from the crawlspace get distributed throughout the home.

5

Cover-up remodeling

Cosmetic renovations can hide water damage, mold, and improper material layering.

6

"Energy efficient" tight construction

Modern airtight homes can trap moisture, VOCs, and combustion gases inside.

7

Smart home systems

Smart meters, wireless lighting, and IoT devices increase RF radiation throughout the home.

8

Municipal water supply

PFAS, heavy metals, and disinfection byproducts may be present above health guidelines.

9

All-LED lighting

High flicker rates and blue light peaks can disrupt sleep, hormones, and circadian rhythm.

10

Nearby cell towers or power lines

External RF and magnetic field sources that cannot be remediated after purchase.
